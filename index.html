Privacy Policy

App: Morning Reset
Developer: Ryan Neil
Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Morning Reset is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in our app.

Data We Collect

Morning Reset stores all user data — including streaks, XP, mission history, and preferences — locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

In-App Purchases

Morning Reset offers optional subscriptions through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. Purchase data is managed by RevenueCat, which processes subscription status on our behalf in accordance with Apple's guidelines.

Notifications

If you grant permission, Morning Reset schedules local notifications on your device to remind you of your morning protocol. These notifications are generated and stored locally on your device. No notification data is transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Morning Reset uses RevenueCat solely to manage subscription entitlements. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data in accordance with their own privacy policy, available at revenuecat.com.

Children's Privacy

Morning Reset is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
